My Country: Nigeria

Nigeria stands as a vibrant cornerstone of West African culture. Home to over 250 ethnic groups, each with their own distinct traditions, from the bronze sculptures of Benin to the uli arts of the Igbos, the colorful adire textiles of the Yoruba people, and the unique architectural styles and leather work of the Hausas. The country's cultural wealth extends beyond its artifacts to its people, known for their warmth, resilience, and entrepreneurial spirit. Nigeria's diverse landscapes range from the savanna grasslands to tropical rainforests, providing a stunning backdrop to its cultural heritage. Nigerian markets burst with artisanal excellence, showcasing handwoven baskets, intricately carved wooden masks, vibrant beadwork, and leather goods that embody generations of skill and creativity.
