Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is Africa’s most populous country and is renowned for its rich cultural diversity. With over 250 ethnic groups, including Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo, Nigeria is home to vibrant music, art, and traditional festivals. Its bustling cities, historic landmarks, and natural beauty make it a cultural and economic hub in Africa.
